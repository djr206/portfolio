Thank you for supporting Washington State Bowhunters, which was founded in 1976 as a non-profit organization that represents all bowhunters of Washington State, whether traditional or compound bowhunters. Washington State Bowhunters promotes bowhunter education and works tirelessly on behalf of all bowhunters to improve archery seasons in Washington State.

We have events for everyone! Our Annual Banquet will be April 13, 2019 in Renton! Join us on July 19, 20, and 21 for our 2019 Rendezvous event in Cle Elum, WA.

Washington State Bowhunters is the leading state organization that goes to bat for all bowhunters with the Washington State Department of Fish and Wildlife, whether traditional or compound bowhunters. Our efforts over the years have given bowhunters parity of harvest with the muzzleloaders and rifle hunters and we continue to pressure the game department to maintain this equality.
